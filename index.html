About Rageni Agro Resort Pvt. Ltd.

Your Gateway to Sustainable Luxury in Baglung

Eco-Tourism & Authentic Experiences

Rageni Agro Resort (RAR) offers eco-tourism and adventure, including trekking to Dhaulagiri and Dhorpatan. Experience authentic rural life, organic Nepali cuisine, and panoramic Himalayan views through homestays.

Baglung, known for its scenic beauty and Magar culture, provides farm stays, organic farming insights, and farm-to-table dining. RAR promotes sustainable development through agriculture, tourism, and quality service.

Our goal is to enhance organic farming, support rural livelihoods, and integrate agro-based tourism. Through our unit, The Silver Palace, we deliver excellent hospitality while showcasing our region's natural and cultural richness.
